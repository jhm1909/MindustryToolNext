# Welcome to the Mindustry Wiki
<br />
The **Mindustry Wiki** is a comprehensive, community-driven knowledge base for the sandbox tower defense game **Mindustry**. Built collaboratively by players and automatically maintained through the **Mindustry Tool**, this wiki serves as your go-to reference for everything from basic gameplay to advanced automation and strategy.  
<br />
> **Current Game Version:** `147.1`
<br />
<br />
## Purpose of This Wiki
<br />
Our goal is to create a well-structured, up-to-date, and detailed resource that helps players of all skill levels get the most out of their Mindustry experience. This includes:  
<br />
<br />
- Clear explanations of core game mechanics: resource production, logistics, combat, and logic systems.  
- Practical guides and strategies to help optimize builds, automation chains, and defenses.  
- In-depth documentation of blocks, units, tech trees, and version updates.  
- A collaborative space for community contributions and open knowledge-sharing.  
<br />
<br />
## Contributing to the Wiki
<br />
This wiki lives on GitHub, and everyone is welcome to contribute!  
<br />
<br />
1. To make changes, click the **Edit** button at the top of any page.  
2. You'll be directed to a pull request form where you can submit your edits.  
3. Once reviewed, your contribution will be merged into the live site.  
<br />
<br />
> **Note:** Much of the content on this wiki is **auto-generated**. If you'd like to edit these auto-generated sections, please submit your changes via a pull request to the  
> **[Mindustry Tool repository](https://github.com/sharrlotte/MindustryToolNext/tree/master/docs)** instead.
